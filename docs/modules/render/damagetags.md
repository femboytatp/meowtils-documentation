# **DamageTags**

#### Displays the damage dealth after hitting someone.

---

## Text color

The color of the damage text.

## Scale

Size of the text.

## Expire

The time text should stay after hitting someone.

## Dynamic color

Changes text color based on amount of damage dealt.

## Fade out

Smoothly fades out the text after hitting someone, until it reaches the expire time.

## Show suffix

Will show a heart at the end of the damage text.
