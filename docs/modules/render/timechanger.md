# **TimeChanger**

#### Allows you to change time clientside.

---

## Time

The time to display, from the 0-24th hour of the day.

## Use real time

Uses your real current time.

## Increase

Keybind to increase the time while held down.

## Decrease

Keybind to decrease the time while held down.
