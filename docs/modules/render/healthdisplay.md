# **HealthDisplay**

#### Displays various health related information.

---

## Show own health

### Enabled

If enabled shows your current HP on screen.

### Scale

The size of your own health text.

## Show bow damage

### Enabled

If enabled shows the damage you dealth to another player after shooting them with a bow.

### Scale

The size of the bow damage text.

### Hide suffix

Removes the "HP" at the end of the bow damage text.
