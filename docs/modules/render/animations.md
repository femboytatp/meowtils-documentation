# **Animations**

#### Visually changes some animations.

---

## Fake autoblock

Visually blocks your sword while swinging. Does not change any gameplay mechanics.

## Activate

### Always

Always block during swing.

### Rightclick

Only block while also holding down rightclick, this polls clicks outside of Minecraft in order to work with other clients such as Vape.

!!! Failure "Important"

    This currently only works on Windows.

### Killaura

This only blocks if you have not recently pressed right or left click but is still swinging your arm.

!!! Failure "Important"

    Similarily to the Rightclick mode, this polls click states outside of Minecraft and currently only works on Windows.

## Cancel swing animation

Visually cancels your swing animation in first person.

### Rightclick only

Only cancels swinging animation while also blocking your sword.

## Cancel consume animation

Visually cancels the animation played while consuming an item such as food.

## Cancel bow animation

Visually cancels the "zooming-in" animation played while currently charging a bow.
