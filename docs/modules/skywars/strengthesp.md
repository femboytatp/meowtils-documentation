# **StrengthESP**

#### Shows an ESP on players that currently have the strength effect.

---

## Mode

### 3D

Renders a 3D esp.

### 2D

Renders a 2D esp.

## ESP color

Change the color of the ESP box.

## Fill entire box

Fills the entire ESP box rather than just having an outline.

## Fade out

Smoothly fades out as time passes, until they no longer have strength.
