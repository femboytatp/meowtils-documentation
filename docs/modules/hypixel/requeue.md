# **Requeue**

#### Easily requeue a game.

---

## Feedback message

Will send a message with relevant information related to the module.

## Auto requeue

Automatically requeues after game end.

## Delay

Delay to wait until automatically requeuing, in seconds.

## Requeue bind

Lets you bind a key that when pressed will requeue for you.

## Commands

/rq - _Requeues last played game._
