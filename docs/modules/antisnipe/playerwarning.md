# **PlayerWarning**

#### Warns you of specific players.

---

## Blacklisted warning

Warns you when a player that is currently in your blacklist exists in your world.

### Blacklist ping sound

Plays a sound for blacklist warnings.

## Sniper warning

Alerts you if a player may be a sniper.

### Sniper ping sound

Plays a sound for sniper warnings.

### Check gear

Checks gear for snipers.

**Applies to:**

- Chainmail Armor + Iron Sword

### Check name

Checks if a player has a blacklisted name, and if so warn them for being a potential sniper.
